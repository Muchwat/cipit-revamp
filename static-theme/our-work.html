Our Research & Impact Areas

We focus on critical intersections of technology, law, and development, providing evidence-based research and policy engagement across several key themes that shape the future of Africa's digital economy.

Core Research Pillars

Artificial Intelligence

Policy Research & Ethics

Analyzing the legal and ethical implications of AI adoption in Kenya and Africa, focusing on fairness, accountability, and transparency in deployment across various sectors.

Data Governance & Policy

Regulatory Frameworks

Investigating local and cross-border data flows, developing policy recommendations for data protection, digital sovereignty, and privacy rights in the digital age.

Digital Identity

Rights & Inclusion

Researching the legal frameworks governing digital identity systems, their impact on inclusion and exclusion, and associated human rights concerns for vulnerable populations.

IP and Innovation

Commercialization Support

Providing analysis on patent law, copyright, trademarks, and how to create an effective legal environment for technology transfer and innovation in the region.

Cyber Law & Policy

Security & Legislation

Focusing on national cybersecurity legislation, combating cybercrime, and the legal challenges posed by digital security threats, surveillance, and critical infrastructure protection.

Digital Trade & E-commerce

Market Access & Regulation

Examining legal and policy issues related to digital trade agreements, consumer protection, and the regulatory environment for cross-border e-commerce in Africa.
